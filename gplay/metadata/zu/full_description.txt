Gomoku esibizwa nangokuthi gobang, Tic Tac Toe noma eziyisihlanu zilandelana is a classic isu ibhodi game for abadlali ababili. Abadlali enye ukuhambisa uphawu umbala babo indlela cell ezingenalutho on ebhodini. Ophumelele yilona umdlali wokuqala ukuthola i irowu ziqotho amamaki emihlanu nendawo, zibheka, noma ngokuphambeneyo

Izici:
* 5 amazinga nobunzima
* Hlehlisa iphutha lakho uhamba
* Khetha ukuthi iyiphi ebhodini bheka kalula nge eziningi ibhodi & background imibala.
* Engeza Vietnamese Caro rule (ukuvimba 2 emaceleni)
* Ukusekela English, Vietnamese
* Yenza iphrofayela yakho nezinhlobo eziningi avatar.
